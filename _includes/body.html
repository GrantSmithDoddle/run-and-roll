Run… and Roll

Sore knees, aching hips, not running due to injury?
The “Run… and Roll” workshop, with practical demonstrations, and the latest evidence, will help you:
  1. Manage “niggles”
  2. Learn why and how to use the foam roller
  3. Understand running “form”
  4. Find out if and when running is bad for you

The Coaches

Julianne
Jameson

Physiotherapist specialising in assessing and training runners. She has trained in running analysis and strength and conditioning. Always reading the latest research or looking at the value of the latest running fad, you could say she is obsessed by all things running!

She has run several road and trail marathons, has run an ultramarathon of 43 miles as well as 5k and 10k races.

Jane
Rackham

Sports therapist and accomplished Ironman triathlete. As well as sports / remedial massage Jane specialises in myofascial release using the Rossiter System method. An advanced level 4 Rossiter coach and only 1 of 9 in the UK. Working in the health and fitness industry for around 17 years, Jane is also qualified fitness and gym instructor.
